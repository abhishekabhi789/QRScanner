Privacy Policy for QRScanner

Introduction

Thank you for using QRScanner! This Privacy Policy explains how this app handles data when you use it.

Data Collection and Usage

  • The app stores scanned and generated codes locally on your device.
  • Users can share and export scanned or generated codes, but QRScanner itself does not share any data or provide cloud storage.
  • The app may be included in Android's backup process, which is handled by the operating system.

Permissions Required

  • Camera Permission: Required to scan QR codes and barcodes.
  • Storage Permission: Needed on certain devices to save and read exported databases.

Third-Party Services

QRScanner does not upload or share data with external services. However, it uses third-party libraries, including:

  • ML Kit (Barcode Scanning API) – for scanning QR codes and barcodes.
  • CameraX – for camera access and QR code scanning.

A full list of third-party licenses is available in the app.

Data Storage & Sharing

  • All scanned and generated data remains on your device unless you choose to share it.
  • The app does not upload data to any servers.

Children's Privacy

QRScanner is not specifically designed for children and does not contain age-restricted content. However, it does not include any child-specific protections. Parents or guardians should supervise usage if necessary.

Future Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated as new features (such as internet-based functionality) are added. The latest version will always be available within the app and on its distribution platform (e.g., Google Play). Please review it periodically for any changes.
